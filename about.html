Who We Are

Strategic Organisational Development

At Intelligent OD, we bring over two decades of experience in transforming organisations through strategic organisational development and change management. Founded by Kash Haroon, our consultancy combines deep expertise across multiple sectors with innovative, evidence-based approaches to create lasting positive change.

As a preferred supplier to the Local Government Association and trusted provider across the local government sector in England, we bring extensive public sector knowledge alongside broader cross-sector insights.

We understand that every organisation faces unique challenges in today's rapidly evolving landscape.

Mission

To be the leading voice in practical and digital OD, helping organisations navigate change with confidence and clarity.

Promise

Supporting organisations to adapt and reinvent, making change work - through trusted partnerships and proven approaches.

Our Approach

Proven Four-Step Framework

What sets us apart is our commitment to creating practical, sustainable solutions through our proven four-step framework:

1. Diagnose: We conduct comprehensive assessments to identify root causes and understand your organisational culture in depth.
2. Decide: Working collaboratively, we use data-driven insights to make informed decisions and validate strategies.
3. Implement: We execute tailored solutions that align with your objectives and create lasting change.
4. Impact: Through careful measurement and evaluation, we ensure meaningful outcomes and continuous improvement.
