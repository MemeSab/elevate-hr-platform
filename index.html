Senior Teaching Fellow & OD Consultant

Kash Haroon

Navigating systemic complexity with over 20 years of experience in transformational change and executive leadership.

Kash Haroon is a highly accomplished Organisational Design & Development (OD) Consultant and Executive Coach. Throughout his career, he has led transformational initiatives across the public, private, and not-for-profit sectors. His extensive portfolio includes senior roles at the prestigious Roffey Park Institute, HS2 Ltd (Europe's largest infrastructure project), North West Employers, and the NHS.

In addition to his consultancy work, Kash shapes the next generation of leaders as a Senior Teaching Fellow at Liverpool John Moores University (Liverpool Business School). Specializing in inclusive culture building, Equality, Diversity & Inclusion (EDI), and psychological safety, Kash empowers global executive boards to build resilient, future-ready organizations.
